About Me

Hi, wellcome to my website! My name is Mitchell. I am a student at Our Lady of the Bay Catholic High School in Collingwood Ontario. I am currently taking a computer science course, and have experience with html, css, javascript and a little bit of python. A few of my hobbies include playing and building guitars, messing around with electronics, and messing around with personal computers. I am interested in linux, opensource operating systems and firmware and networking.


I plan to take a year off following the end of this school year to work and gain experience. I then plan to go to Conestoga College for a powerline technician program. The program length is two years and has a co op option, in which students earn on average ten thousand dollars per term.


I have not quite made up my mind on what I will be doing for my cct. I have done web development before but have I know that this wil be a new challenge and I have never used databases. I am also interested in learning Swift. I do not have a mac but I know that a swift toolchain has been compiled for windows. I beleive I am leaning more towards swift since it will be an absolutly new language.
