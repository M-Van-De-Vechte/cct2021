Scratch

This is my scratch project. I have done scratch since I was in grade six but they have since add new features and updates so it was very fun to play with it again. I made a game where your decisions affect the outcome. I won't spoil the game but chose how you play wisely. I spent quite a bit of time on this game, but I really enjoyed it. I may end up making a much more refined version of this game on my own time with better animations and chachacters and choices. I believe that although making games where your decisions affect the outcome can be harder to make, there are more possibilities and will encourage the user to play again to try other endings and outcomes.
